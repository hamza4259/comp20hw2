The Gym Buddy Algorithm

The Gym Buddy Matching Algorithm was carefully crafted to match perfect gym partners. The algorithm takes in height, weight, amount of weight to be lifted and other optional parameters to carefully match together partners.

Behind the mind of the algorithm

Takes in height and weight so you can be matched to partners who are physically as capable as you are.

Takes in genetic history so you can be matched to partners who are genetically similar to you.

If you want to gain strength and mass the algorithm will also take that into consideration.

If you want to cut weight and do more cardio the algorithm will also take into consideration.

If you are a beginner to the gym, the algorithm will try to match you with other beginners so you aren't afraid of going to the gym and can get more comfortable!
